Context Engineering Starts With Systems, Not Prompts

A public research lab exploring how AI systems work in public infrastructure and high-stakes contexts.

Read the Guide →

I work on public mechanics—how public systems function, who can see them, and how they serve everyone. As AI becomes embedded in public infrastructure, it needs the same standards of visibility and accountability. State Capacity AI explores context engineering design through that lens: building AI systems that work like good public infrastructure, not black boxes.
